In N Out Sauce

Description

Unofficial recipes taste better than the real thing

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 3 tablespoon plus 2.5 teaspoons ketchup (scant 1/4 cup)
  • 1 teaspoon yellow mustard
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons dill pickle relish
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons sweet pickle relish
  • 1 teaspoon worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon white wine vinegar

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, stir together mayo, ketchup, mustard, sweet and dill relish, worcester sauce, and vinegar
  2. This makes about 1/2 cup sauce. Sauce will keep, covered and refridgerated, for up to 2 weeks
  3. Spread generously on your burger
  4. Eat and enjoy!
